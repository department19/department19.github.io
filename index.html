I am Jack Lam

Aspiring Software Developer

Languages currently learning

My journey into coding began in April 2023, when I started my self-taught path to learn programming. Through the use of resources like freeCodeCamp and MDN, I have learnt a fundamental proficiency in essential web development languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Although my academic background initially revolved around paramedicine, a friend recommended software development to me after I made the decision to drop out of University. His suggestion and advice allowed me to explore a new avenue in life and aligned perfectly with my long-standing interest in tech.

Beyond my passion for tech and my prior dabble into medicine, volleyball has become my favorite sport, capturing my interest with its blend of teamwork and strategy. Whether it's a friendly match with friends or participating in organized match, volleyball provides me with an outlet for competition and self-improvement with others.

With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I would like to further develop my front-end development skills and eager to dive deeper into other programming languages, frameworks, and tools as well as back-end development. Hence I am currently seeking opportunities in apprenticeships, bootcamps and projects with my friends that allow me to apply my knowledge and continue my professional development.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about me. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and look forward to the rewarding journey of becoming an accomplished software developer!
